Guilt

Guilt should be met with one of two responses. Not how to continue the perpetual resent, but instead the choice between: how this 'mistake' has been of use to me and how this 'mistake' has been of use to others.

If it was useful to you, the lesson will in due course become clear, remembered, and at best this mistake is never repeated again.

If it was useful to others they may not know it at first, but however it may be that you are either hated, reviled or silently judged so be their lesson and your reward. Try reasonably to correct a wrong understanding, apologize sincerely or say nothing if needed. Be skillful, but don't resent.
