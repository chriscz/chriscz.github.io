Chris — Sat 26 May 2018

Dee

Dee

I miss your touch
our warm embrace
A sweet dream small
unerased

The memories linger
like a stick through thick
taking its time to re-adjust
to the state that was before

It's early morn as the rain touches down,
and I lay here without my crown:
My queen has left
to god knows where
but the fire in her heart
will take her there

She's an unchained spirit:
now here,
now there.
Free from the fetters of average men
her wishes and visions lie beyond plain conception
their motivations
are close to The Source

Your travels outward have taken you closer
to the fountain of truth and wonder
that fills our hearts with mortal strive.

--

Time you creature of awe
our ends as this lie in your hands
our dance of unknowing
when you'll blow the kiss
that takes us back to the embark.

Sometimes not knowing
gives comfort within
still each moment could be the last.
Yet you know full well
none you can keep
so you dance on the tip of a pin
-- a fine sharp edge --
and become the unfolding
of a pattern so deep
no dream could be more real.
