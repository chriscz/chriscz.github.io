Simple Hummus

Ingredients

  • A can of chick peas drained and rinsed (410g nett mass / 252g drained)
  • a small clove of garlic (~1.5cm in length), or if you really like garlic a clove of twice that length.
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp of lemon juice
  • 2 - 4 tbsp water
  • 1/4 - 1/2 tsp salt (or more to taste)
  • 3/4 - 1 tsp ground cumin

Preparation

  • Add all the ingredients into a blender and blend or
  • Add all ingredients to a bowl and blend using a stick blender (and a lot of effort)

Tips

  • Keep the chick pea water and make some delicious mayo.
