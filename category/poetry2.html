Each passing moment I hope for more,
but my psyche resists:
I've lost this war.

This life unfolds in this dark night
of my soul.
I question: where will I be whole?
No abode offers …

Birth

The wheel has come to turn
to a monumental point.
A mind beyond what was,
has let the waters flow
from a source before untapped.

I sip slowly, as if to ensure
that a future thirst is quenched.
Sadly life waits for no-one,
and soon before long
the stream dries …

The Well

The thirsty soul
stares down the well and shouts;
soon to realize his fault:
the echoed voice his own,
the seed of confusion
the truth of delusion --
while others water their garden.

Forward?

Alone upon this path I stand
flickering candle in my hand.
A mutter on my lips:
"Is this all there is?"
A whisper in my mind
(as the sun sets over the ocean)
where am I from?
where do I go?
this water is too deep to swim.
