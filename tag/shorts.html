Shorts

Mad mind body
bound by belief
Lost in its triumphs
bound for defeat

Gathering moments
as lid to get paid
Or like chocolates to eat
when the years run dry

The ironic meaning of life
lies between the action of deciding
and the belief in having had a choice.
