Molasses Bread

Recently I've been trying my hand at baking bread. Here is a slightly adapted recipe taken from a post by Bridget Oland.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 1 cup rolled oats
    • BEST Large Jungle oats (rolled)
    • OK Normal Jungle oats (quick oats)
    • NO Instant Jungle oats (well, instant)
    • Look here for images.
  • 2 Tbsp butter (replace with margarine for a Vegan alternative)
  • 3 tsp salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 20g dry yeast powder
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 2/3 cup (220g/167ml) or (150ml if you like it less sweet) of molasses
  • 7 cups of flour
  • 3 Unsifted (stone ground) whole wheat flour
  • 4 Whole Wheat brown bread flour

Preparation

  • In a large bowl (you'll be making the dough in it later) mix the oats, butter and salt with the 2 cups of boiling water
  • Let stand till the mixture has become lukewarm.

Activate the yeast

  • After the oats has cooled,
  • use a second bowl and dissolve sugar in 1 cup lukewarm water,
  • add the yeast and mix thoroughly,
  • let it stand for about a minute or two
  • then mix it evenly into the cooled oats mixture.
  • Now stir in the molasses till dissolved.

Oil the pans

  • Sufficiently oil the bread pan(s).
  • You may need two if your pans are small.

Putting it all together

  • Begin adding your whole wheat flour to the mixture,
  • continue mixing till all of it has been added.
  • Next start adding your brown bread flour.
  • I've found that the dough remained far too sticky to knead, so instead continue stirring the mixture, moving what is on the outside of the mixture to the centre for 5-10 minutes.

Let it sit

  • Place the dough into baking trays,
  • let it rise in a warm place for 1:30 - 2:00 hours, or till about doubled in size.

Bake it

  • 5 minutes before baking, heat oven to 180C (350F).
  • Bake for around 30-45 minutes.

Finishing touches

  • Turn the loaf (still in its tray) on one side, covering the top with a clean dish cloth.
  • let stand for 10 minutes, then
  • turn it on the other side and repeat.
  • Remove from tray and let it cool.
